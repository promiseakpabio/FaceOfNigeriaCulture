Face Of Nigeria Cultural Heritage 2026 – Call for Sponsors and Collaborators

The Face of Nigeria Cultural Heritage 2026 is a cultural pageant and empowerment platform dedicated to celebrating Nigeria's rich traditions, beauty, diversity, and values.

This initiative seeks to promote cultural awareness, national unity, tourism, and youth empowerment through the preservation and showcase of Nigeria's unique heritage.

As preparations for the 2026 edition begin, the organization invites sponsors, partners and collaborators from various sectors including fashion, beauty, media, tourism, hospitality, transportation and digital content creation.

Through strategic partnerships, the platform aims to create a memorable event that contributes positively to cultural development while providing opportunities for businesses and organizations to gain visibility.
