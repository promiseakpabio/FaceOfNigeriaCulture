ELIGIBILITY & PARTICIPATION CRITERIA

To be eligible to participate in the Face Of Nigeria Cultural Heritage, applicants must meet the following requirements:

✅ Nationality & Residency
  • Applicant must be a Nigerian citizen or a permanent resident of Nigeria.
  • Applicant must be able to provide valid identification upon request.
✅ Age Requirement
  • Applicant must be between 18 and 28 years old as at the date of the competition.
✅ Marital & Maternal Status
  • Applicant must be single.
  • Applicant must never have been married.
  • Applicant must not have given birth.
✅ Physical & Health Requirements
  • Applicant must be of sound mind, body, and health.
  • Minimum height requirement: 170 cm (without heels).
  • Body size must not exceed US Size 10 (UK size 12)
✅ Educational Qualification
  • No specific educational qualification is required.
  • However, contestants are expected to be articulate, intelligent, and capable of representing Nigeria nationally and internationally.
✅ Personal Conduct & Appearance
  • While in the camp and throughout participation in the Face Of Nigeria Cultural Heritage, contestants must understand that:
  • They are public representatives of the Face Of Nigeria Cultural Heritage brand.
  • It is their personal responsibility to dress, speak, and behave in a manner consistent with how they wish to be perceived. Decency, respect, and professionalism are expected at all times.
✅ Application Materials
  • Applicants must submit:
  • A completed application form
  • Three (3) recent photographs (clear, unedited, and current)
  • Failure to submit required materials will result in automatic disqualification.
✅Travel & Tour Commitment
  • Applicants must be willing and available to travel within Nigeria.
  • Applicants must participate in state tours, camp activities, media engagements, and official assignments as directed by the organization.
✅ General Agreement
  • By applying, the contestant agrees to:
  • Abide by all rules, guidelines and decisions of the Face Of Nigeria Cultural Heritage Organization.
  • Participate fully in all official activities associated with the competition.
